what is brochill?

Brochill is a vernacular content platform. It is a personalizable content discovery platform. Creators post personalisable images and videos, and users consume them. All content is curated based on trends, events and festivals.

what do we do?

BroChill provides a platform for innovative content creators. Where creators can post personalizable videos, personalizable images, and short videos on BroChill. All content posted by creators is curated based on user interests, social ratings, trends, events, special days, and festivals.
